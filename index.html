Windfarm Polska III sp. z o.o. :

Windfarm Polska III Sp. z o.o. (WFP III) is a Polish company owning the Jasna Onshore Windfarm located in Sztum district, Poland. WFP III is wholly owned by Stadtwerke MÃ¼nchen GmbH (SWM), in turn being a 100% daughter company of the City of Munich. The windfarm has an installed capacity of 132 MW using 17x 3,3 MW and 22x 3,45 MW Vestas V126 turbines. It will be fully commissioned in 1 January 2021, and the start-up phase begins in April 2020.
